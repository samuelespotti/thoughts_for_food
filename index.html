Thoughts For Food

The Thoughts For Food magazine wants to provide the users with different perspectives from which to read about food.

Science, history, dietology and many other fields of human knowledge are here to enrich your view about food.

Whether you are a cook or a nutritionist or simply a food-lover, you must check this magazine out! We have enough dishes for every taste!

Issues

Only for you here are three issues just released :

  1. Cultivated meet: between costs and health
  2. Vegan, Low-calorie and Mediterranean diet
  3. Food and diets through history

Each one of them addresses its own specific topic from different perspectives to provide you with as reacher insight as possible.

Typographic themes

The Thoughts For Food magazine hides some magic inside it... You can switch from a graphic style to another only by clicking on the clock icon!
Each one of the themes was built according to a specific historic graphic style (you can check the building process on our documentation page).
So, choose the right theme and get ready for a travel back (and forward...) in time!

  • 1400s - Gutemberg Bible Theme
  • 1800s - Kelsmcott Press Theme
  • 1920s - Dadaism Theme
  • 1980s - Aironi Magazine Theme
  • 2000s - Pop Rock Bands Theme
  • 2030s - Futuristic Theme
